## Welcome!

Hello! I'm **Mert Kaplandar**, an Electrical and Electronics Engineering student with a strong interest in **embedded systems**, **low-level software development**, and **hardware design**.

My passion lies in designing reliable and efficient embedded systems by combining software with electronics. I enjoy learning how hardware and software interact at a low level and continuously improving my engineering skills through hands-on projects and practical experience.

This website serves as my academic and technical portfolio, where I document my projects, research interests, and learning journey as I work toward becoming an embedded systems engineer.
